About This Course

Commanding Discourse teaches you 30 expressions across 6 discourse strategies, building a complete architecture for sophisticated professional speaking. Each lesson adds 5 key expressions and drills them under time pressure alongside expressions from previous lessons.

The course follows the Discourse Flow Sequence: FRAME → POSITION → NAVIGATE → QUALIFY → CONNECT → COMMAND. Each step builds on the last. By the capstone, you'll deploy all 30 expressions fluidly under pressure. Every lesson includes spaced retrieval, interleaved drills, and a 10-second pressure recall mechanism.

Course Lessons

⏳ 1 FRAME C1
Discourse Mastery
Master 5 FRAME expressions for opening with composure: buying time, weighing up, introducing possibilities, reframing, and adding caveats. The foundation of every sophisticated response.
📍 2 POSITION C1
Argument Structure
Master 5 POSITION expressions for building arguments: stating your stance, citing evidence, explaining implications, acknowledging counterpoints, and strengthening reasoning.
🤝 3 NAVIGATE C1
Diplomatic Disagreement
Master 5 NAVIGATE expressions for handling pushback: acknowledging then redirecting, offering alternatives, pushing back diplomatically, playing devil's advocate, and agreeing to disagree.
🔮 4 QUALIFY C1
Speculative Language
Master 5 QUALIFY expressions for calibrating certainty: introducing possibilities, hypothetical consequences, expressing probability, keeping options open, and forecasting outcomes.
🔗 5 CONNECT C1
Cohesion & Flow
Master 5 CONNECT expressions for sustained turns: building on points, circling back, synthesising themes, naming throughlines, and elevating to broader principles.
⚔️ 6 COMMAND Capstone
High-Stakes Communication
The capstone. Master 5 COMMAND expressions for pressure moments: directness, bottom-line clarity, making asks, proposing solutions, and summarising takeaways. Drills all 30 expressions.
