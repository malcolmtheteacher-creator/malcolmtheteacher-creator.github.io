About Series 2

Series 2 continues the Curious Conversations approach with six more fascinating topics designed to spark genuine curiosity and create natural, engaging discussions. Each lesson focuses on universal human experiences that everyone has opinions about.

What makes these lessons work: Real research, real stories, 5 focused expressions per lesson, student choice in discussion topics, and content that makes you forget you're in an English lesson.
