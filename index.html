How a lesson works

Read a little. Speak a lot.

The same simple shape every time — built so that you do almost all the talking.

1

A story worth telling

A great short story, or a true moment from history — something you actually want to talk about.

2

The words you need

The key vocabulary first, in context, so the text (and the grammar in it) suddenly makes sense.

3

Read & notice

Read it in parts, with a light grammar focus drawn from the text itself — never a dry rule.

4

Then you speak

The heart of it: timed speaking tasks that turn the story into real, confident conversation.
