‚úì Responses to Suggestions:

Positive: "Yes, let's!" / "That's a great idea!" / "I'd love to!"
Decline Politely: "That sounds nice, but I can't." / "I appreciate the suggestion, but..."

Model Dialogues

Model 1: Friends (Casual)

A: "Let's go to the beach tomorrow!"

B: "Yes, let's! That sounds fun!"

Model 2: Colleagues (Polite)

A: "Why don't we have a meeting on Tuesday?"

B: "That works well for me. Let's do that."

Model 3: Formal (Respectful)

A: "Shall we start the presentation?"

B: "Yes, of course. Let's begin."

Model 4: Declining Politely

A: "Let's go to the gym together."

B: "I appreciate the invitation, but I'm busy today."

‚úì Notice: The person declining APOLOGIZES and offers A REASON. Not rude, just honest.

Mixed Scenarios

Identify formality and respond appropriately:

Scenario 1: Your manager asks: "Shall we review the project?"

This is FORMAL. Respond politely.

Scenario 2: Your friend says: "Let's go for a walk!"

This is CASUAL. Respond naturally.

Scenario 3: Your colleague asks: "Why don't we try a new approach?"

This is POLITE/PROFESSIONAL. Agree or decline respectfully.
