üò£ Describing How You Feel
"I have a...": headache, backache, stomachache, toothache, fever, cough, sore throat
"I feel...": sick, ill, dizzy, tired, weak, nauseous, pain (in my...)
"My [body part] hurts" / "My [body part] is sore": "My head hurts." "My back is sore."
"I'm [adjective]": fine, well, better, worse, okay
