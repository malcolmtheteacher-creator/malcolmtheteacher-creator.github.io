üìç Let's Learn: Prepositions of Location

Prepositions tell you WHERE something is. These 5 are essential:

1. IN - inside a container/space

Examples:

  • "The book is in the bag."
  • "I live in Brazil."
  • "The dog is in the house."
2. ON - touching the surface of something

Examples:

  • "The cup is on the table."
  • "The picture is on the wall."
  • "My keys are on the desk."
3. UNDER - below something

Examples:

  • "The cat is under the bed."
  • "My shoes are under the table."
  • "The bridge goes under the river."
4. NEXT TO - beside something, very close

Examples:

  • "I sit next to the window."
  • "The bank is next to the supermarket."
  • "My brother sleeps next to me."
5. BETWEEN - in the middle of two things

Examples:

  • "The school is between the park and the hospital."
  • "I sit between my two friends."
  • "The pen is between the books."
üí° Pattern: [Object] + is + [preposition] + [location]. Example: "The phone is ON the table."
