Frequency words make you sound more natural and detailed!

üìä The Frequency Adverbs Scale

Here are the main frequency adverbs, ordered from most to least:

ALWAYS 100% of the time Example: "I always eat breakfast"
USUALLY About 80-90% of the time Example: "I usually wake up at 7"
OFTEN About 60-70% of the time Example: "I often go to the gym"
SOMETIMES About 30-50% of the time Example: "I sometimes eat pizza"
RARELY About 10-20% of the time Example: "I rarely drink alcohol"
NEVER 0% of the time Example: "I never skip breakfast"

Also useful:

  • Every day / Every week / Every month ‚Äî Frequency expressions (100%)
  • Once a week / Twice a month / Three times a year ‚Äî Specific frequencies

üìç Where Do Frequency Adverbs Go?

This is important for correct English!

Rule 1: WITH "BE" (is, are, was, were)

Subject + BE + FREQUENCY + Rest

  • "I am always happy."
  • "She is usually late."

Rule 2: WITH OTHER VERBS (eat, go, work, play)

Subject + FREQUENCY + Verb + Rest

  • "I always eat breakfast."
  • "She usually goes to work."
  • "We often play football."

Rule 3: AT START OR END (For emphasis)

  • Usually, I go home at 5pm. (START)
  • I go home at 5pm, usually. (END ‚Äî less common)
