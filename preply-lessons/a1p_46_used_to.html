Today we're working on: The "used to" structure for past habits and routines that changed ‚Äî so you can talk about how you've changed over time.

üîë The Core Idea

Used to + verb: Something you did regularly in the past but don't do anymore.

"I used to play tennis every week." (past habit, not true now)
"We used to live in London." (past reality, changed)
"She didn't use to like coffee." (past, but now she does)
