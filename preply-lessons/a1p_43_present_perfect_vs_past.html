Today we're working on: The KEY difference between Present Perfect (life experience) and Past Simple (finished time) ‚Äî so you can choose the RIGHT tense for what you're saying.

üîë The Main Idea

Past Simple: When did it happen? (A specific time in the past)

Present Perfect: Has it ever happened? (Anytime in your life, or recently)

This is THE MOST IMPORTANT distinction for advanced A1 students.
