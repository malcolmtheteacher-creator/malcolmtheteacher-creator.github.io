🎯 Framework 1: GREET-COMPANY-PRODUCT-CONNECT

When: When you meet someone at a trade fair booth or event

How:

1. GREET — Welcome + your name + your position

2. COMPANY — What your company does (one sentence)

3. PRODUCT — Your main product or service + one adjective

4. CONNECT — Ask about THEM

Goal: Make a confident first impression in 60 seconds

📱 Framework 2: W-W-W (WHAT-WHY-WHO)

When: When a visitor asks about your product or you're explaining what you make

How:

1. WHAT — What is your product? Describe it clearly.

2. WHY — Why is it good? What makes it different?

3. WHO — Who needs it? Who are your customers?

Goal: Answer any product question thoroughly

👋 Framework 3: EXIT (End-eXchange-Invite-Thank)

When: When you're ready to end a conversation and exchange contact information

How:

1. End — Know when the conversation is finishing

2. eXchange — Exchange business cards or contact details

3. Invite — Invite them to stay in touch or visit your website

4. Thank — Thank them for their time

Goal: End professionally and build the relationship

☕ Framework 4: SAFE (Surroundings-Activities-Food-Events)

When: During small talk with people you're just meeting (lunch, networking dinner, coffee break)

How:

1. Surroundings — "Have you been to this city before?"

2. Activities — "Did you catch any interesting presentations?"

3. Food — "Are you enjoying the food here?"

4. Events — "Is this your first time at this fair?"

Goal: Have relaxed conversation and build connection

🆘 Framework 5: CALM (Clarify-Apologise-Look-Move)

When: When there's a complaint, problem, or misunderstanding

How:

1. Clarify — Ask what the problem is. Listen carefully.

2. Apologise — Say sorry professionally. Show you understand.

3. Look — Offer solutions. "What I can do is..."

4. Move — End on a positive note. Thank them.

Goal: Turn a problem into a stronger relationship

✓ SAFE (start with small talk — about the dinner, the fair, the city)

Simulation 3: Problem Scenario

This combines EVERYTHING. A visitor comes to your booth, but something is wrong.

Your company: SeaBridge Logistics — shipping solutions. You're the Account Manager. You work with a customer who has a problem.

🎬 The Scene

"I ordered 50 containers through your company 2 weeks ago. The contract said delivery in 3 weeks. But I just heard from my warehouse — nothing arrived yet! This is a disaster. I have 20 customers waiting for their orders. What are you going to do about this?"

You need to use ALL frameworks:

✓ CALM (handle the problem immediately)

✓ Then move to rebuilding: SAFE (calm them down), W-W-W (rebuild trust in your service)

✓ EXIT (end professionally, they trust you again)
