üìã SCENARIO: Weekend Trip to Paris

You're planning a weekend trip to Paris. Here's what you need to do (using Unit 4 skills):

  1. Phone the travel agent (Lesson 32): Call to ask about flight options. Introduce yourself, state your reason for calling, ask questions.
  2. Book a restaurant (Lesson 31): Once in Paris, call a restaurant. Request a table, ask about dietary options, confirm the booking.
  3. Weather planning (Lesson 36): Talk about the weather forecast. Is it sunny or rainy? How will this affect your plans?
  4. First conditional thinking (Lesson 33): Create backup plans. "If it rains, I'll visit museums." "If there's no availability, I'll try a different restaurant."
  5. Health check (Lesson 37): Before traveling, describe how you feel. Are you ready to go?
  6. Polite requests (Lesson 39): Use "Could" when asking for help: "Could you recommend a hotel?" "Would you suggest a good restaurant?"
