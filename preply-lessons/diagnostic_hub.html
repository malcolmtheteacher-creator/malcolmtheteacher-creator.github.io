How It Works

Each level uses a different game to test your grammar — from quick-fire traffic lights at A1 to academic editing at C2. Every diagnostic has four stages:

  • Getting Started: Quick warm-up to get you thinking at the right level
  • The Game: A different challenge at every level — bingo, detective work, precision picks, and more
  • Review: See every error explained with the grammar rule behind it
  • Your Results: A breakdown by grammar category with links to lessons that target your weak areas

Complete one level per session. Start where you think your level is — or go from A1 upwards for a full grammar profile.
