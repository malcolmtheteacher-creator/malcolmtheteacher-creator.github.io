Why does this matter?

🤝
The first 60 seconds at a trade fair booth decide everything.

A visitor stops at your stand. You have one minute to make a good impression — in English. What do you say? How do you start? How do you describe what your company does?

Thousands of exhibitors, hundreds of visitors — the people who can explain clearly and confidently get the best contacts.

Step 1: The Greeting

At a trade fair, you need a clear, professional greeting. Look at these two versions:

❌ Unclear

"Hi. I am here. I have company."

✅ Clear & Professional

"Hello! Welcome to our stand. I'm Anna, the CEO of BrightLine Solutions."

What differences do you notice? Which sounds more professional? Why?

The Framework: 4 Steps to a Great First Contact

1
GREET — Welcome + your name + your position
2
COMPANY — What your company does (one sentence)
3
PRODUCT — Your main product or service (one sentence)
4
CONNECT — Ask about THEM

Useful Phrases — Step 1: GREET

👋
"Hello! Welcome to our stand."
A warm, professional first line at your booth
🏷
"I'm [name], the [position] of [company]."
Clear introduction — name + job title + company name
😊
"Nice to meet you." / "Good to see you."
Simple, polite follow-up

Useful Phrases — Step 2: COMPANY

🏢
"We are a [type] company from [country]."
Example: "We are a technology company from Spain."
⚙️
"We make / produce / sell [product]."
What your company does — one clear sentence
🌍
"We work with clients in [region/number of countries]."
Shows the size and reach of your business

Useful Phrases — Steps 3 & 4: PRODUCT + CONNECT

⭐
"Our main product is [name]. It's [adjective]."
One product + one strong adjective (natural, premium, innovative...)
🔄
"And you? What company are you from?"
Turn the conversation — ask about the other person
❓
"What brings you to the fair?"
Great open question — works with everyone

Notice: Thomas used all 4 steps. He kept it short. He asked about the other person at the end.

Responding when THEY introduce themselves

At a trade fair, you don't only introduce yourself — you also need to respond when someone introduces their company. Here are useful responses:

👍
"That sounds interesting!"
🤔
"How long have you been in this market?"
🌐
"Do you work mainly in Europe, or internationally?"
Imagine someone just introduced their company. Use one of these responses and then ask a follow-up question of your own.
