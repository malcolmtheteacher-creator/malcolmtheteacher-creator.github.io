👔 BONUS: Complete Formal vs. Casual Register Guide

Throughout these 50 lessons, you've learned that register (formality level) is CRUCIAL. Here's your speak about A1 register reference:

Situation 💬 Casual (Friends/Family) 👔 Formal (Professional/Academic)
Greeting Hey! What's up? How ya doing? Good morning. How do you do? Pleased to meet you.
Asking for help Can you help me? Can you do that? Could you please assist me? Would you be able to help?
Past experiences I've been there like three times. Really cool! I have visited on multiple occasions. Quite impressive.
Opinions I think it's awesome! Really good. In my opinion, it's quite excellent. I would say...
Making plans Wanna grab coffee? I'm gonna be free Saturday. Would you like to have coffee? I will be available Saturday.
Disagreement Nah, I don't think so. You're wrong, man. I respectfully disagree. I don't believe that's accurate.
Giving advice You should totally do it! Why don't you try? I would recommend that action. Have you considered...?
Thanking Thanks a lot! Cheers, mate! Thank you very much. I appreciate your assistance.

Key Register Markers:

  • Casual: Contractions (I've, don't, wanna), short spoken responses, slang, exclamation marks, friendly tone
  • Formal: Full forms (I have, I would), longer spoken responses, complex vocabulary, proper punctuation, respectful tone
