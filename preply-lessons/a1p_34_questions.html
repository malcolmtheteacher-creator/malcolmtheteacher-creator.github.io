üéØ Interleaved Practice

Each question requires you to CHOOSE the right form. Mix of question types and tenses:

1. Form a question to ask if someone is coming tomorrow (future with will):

"__________ you __________ tomorrow?"

Answer: "Will you come tomorrow?"

2. Form a yes/no question in present simple:

"__________ they live in London?"

Answer: "Do they live in London?"

3. Form a modal question (Can/Could):

"__________ you help me?"

Answer: "Can you help me?" or "Could you help me?"

4. Form a WH-question about when (past simple):

"When __________ you __________ home?"

Answer: "When did you come home?"
