üìö Why Irregular Verbs Matter

Look at this story:

"Yesterday, I woke up late. I ate breakfast quickly. I ran to the bus. I sat down and felt tired. But I had a good day!"

Did you notice? These verbs DON'T use -ED! They change completely:

  • WAKE ‚Üí WOKE (not "waked")
  • EAT ‚Üí ATE (not "eated")
  • RUN ‚Üí RAN (not "runned")
  • SIT ‚Üí SAT (not "sitted")
  • FEEL ‚Üí FELT (not "feeled")
  • HAVE ‚Üí HAD (not "haved")

These are IRREGULAR verbs. They're broken, unusual, special ‚Äî and they're VERY common. The more you use English, the more you'll recognize them naturally.
