üïê Let's Learn: Time Expressions

To describe routines, you need words for WHEN you do things:

Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Night

  • "I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning."
  • "I have lunch at noon / 12 o'clock."
  • "I work in the afternoon."
  • "I watch TV in the evening."

Days of the Week

  • Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
  • "I always work on Monday."
  • "I never work on Sunday."

Frequency Adverbs (How often?)

  • Always = every time, 100%
  • Usually = most times, ~80%
  • Often = many times, ~60%
  • Sometimes = occasionally, ~40%
  • Never = 0%, not at all
üí° Pattern: "I [frequency adverb] [verb]" ‚Üí "I always wake up at 7" / "I never eat breakfast"
