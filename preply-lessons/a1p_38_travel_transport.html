üé´ Booking & Tickets

üß≥ Travel Conversations
Planning a trip: "I'm going to..." / "I'm traveling to..." / "I'm leaving on Monday" / "I'll be there for 3 days"
At a station/airport: "Where's the ticket counter?" / "Which platform for the train to...?" / "Is this the right gate?"
On transport: "Is this seat taken?" / "Excuse me, I need to get off" / "Do you know how long until...?"
Questions about journey: "How long is the flight?" / "Are there delays?" / "Do we have a layover?"
