Sequencing Words (Part 1)

2Ô∏è‚É£ Then / After that / Next

Time Markers (Part 2)

‚è∞ When Did It Happen?

Yesterday: "I went to the beach yesterday."

Last week: "I saw my friend last week."

Last year: "I visited Japan last year."

Time markers help your listener know WHEN the story happened.

‚úì Complete Story:

Yesterday, I had an interesting day. First, I woke up late. Then, I went to the cafe. After that, I met my friend. Finally, I went home at 6 PM.
