üìä The Big Picture: Three Tenses

You now know three ways to talk about time:

TENSE FORM WHEN TO USE EXAMPLE
Present Simple Base verb (I work) Every day, habits, now "I work every day."
Past Simple Verb + -ED or irregular (worked, went) Already finished, yesterday "I worked yesterday." / "I went home."
Going To (Future) Be + going to + verb (am/is/are going to work) Plans, decisions, tomorrow "I'm going to work tomorrow."

How to CHOOSE the right tense:

  • Is it happening NOW or EVERY DAY? ‚Üí Use PRESENT
  • Did it ALREADY HAPPEN? ‚Üí Use PAST (regular -ED or irregular)
  • Is it a FUTURE PLAN? ‚Üí Use "GOING TO"
