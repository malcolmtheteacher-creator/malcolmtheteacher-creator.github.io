The Body Peg System: Your First Memory Palace

Now that you can create vivid images, we need a LOCATION SYSTEM to store them in order. The Body Peg system uses 10 parts of your body as fixed locations.

How It Works:

1. Assign each body part a number (1-10) - always the same ones

2. When you get a list of items, place each item on the corresponding body part

3. Create vivid, bizarre interactions between the item and the body part

4. To recall, walk through your body from top to bottom

For each body part, we'll place a memorable image to lock in that location:

These anchors make the locations MORE vivid, which helps anchor your TO-BE-REMEMBERED items to them!

Elaboration: Why Body Pegs Work

Question 1: Why use your body instead of random locations?

Why is your body more reliable than trying to imagine a random room?

Sample: Your body is always with you, familiar, and unchanging. You don't have to memorize it. Random rooms fade and become confused. Your body is automatic.

Question 2: Why place items ON the body part instead of just linking them?

What's the advantage of physically placing a sandwich on your HEAD?

Sample: It creates a multi-sensory experience. You SEE it, FEEL it, maybe SMELL it. This engages more brain systems and creates stronger encoding than just linking concepts.
