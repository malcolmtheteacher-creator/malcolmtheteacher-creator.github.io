Memory Systems Comparison

Method of Loci

Best for: Large lists, speeches, vivid imagery. Technique: Associate items with physical locations. Power: Leverages spatial memory, one of our strongest cognitive abilities.

Spaced Repetition

Best for: Long-term retention, facts, languages. Technique: Review material at increasing intervals. Power: Optimizes timing to prevent forgetting curve.

Elaborative Encoding

Best for: Deep understanding, complex concepts. Technique: Connect new info to existing knowledge. Power: Creates rich neural networks for recall.

Chunking

Best for: Numbers, sequences, codes. Technique: Group information into meaningful units. Power: Expands working memory capacity.

Story Method

Best for: Narrative sequences, events, relationships. Technique: Weave items into a coherent story. Power: Harnesses narrative memory strength.

Peg System

Best for: Lists with specific order, rankings. Technique: Attach items to pre-memorized "pegs". Power: Enables flexible retrieval and reordering.
