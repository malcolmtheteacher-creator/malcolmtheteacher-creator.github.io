üìö SMASHIN' SCOPE: 12 Ways to Make Images Stick

üîë The Secret to Vivid Memory

Your brain is a visual machine. A BORING image ‚Üí you forget it in seconds. A WILD, VIVID image ‚Üí you remember it for LIFE.

SMASHIN' SCOPE gives you 12 tools to transform boring into UNFORGETTABLE:

üîÑ Substitution

Replace one thing with another

Instead of imagining a DESK, imagine your FACE sitting at the desk, wearing a tie.

‚ö° Movement

Make things move, jump, dance

Instead of a STATUE standing still, imagine it DANCING wildly, kicking its legs.

üé¨ Action

Make things DO something dramatic

Instead of a BOOK sitting quietly, imagine it FLYING through the air and EXPLODING into confetti.

üìè Size

Make it HUGE or tiny

Instead of normal APPLE, imagine an APPLE the size of a house, rolling down the street.

üòÇ Humour

Make it ridiculous or funny

Instead of a normal SHOE, imagine a shoe HONKING like a goose every time you step in it.

üí° Imagination

Combine impossible things

Imagine a FISH riding a BICYCLE through the sky while playing VIOLIN.

üî¢ Numbers

Add numbers or calculations

If you see a number, imagine it as a HUGE digit made of that object (like the number 5 made of BANANAS).

üëÉ Senses

Smell, taste, touch, sound

Instead of just seeing PIZZA, imagine TASTING it, SMELLING it, BURNING your mouth on it.

üé® Colour

Make it BRIGHT and UNUSUAL

Instead of a brown TREE, imagine a NEON PURPLE and ELECTRIC BLUE tree.

üìç Order

Place things in a specific location

Instead of random placement, imagine the CAR is PARKED on the ROOF of your house.

üòä Positive Emotion

Make it joyful, happy, exciting

Instead of sad, imagine yourself LAUGHING OUT LOUD and GRINNING with the image.

‚ö° Exaggeration

Go over the top with everything

Instead of normal, imagine a MOUNTAIN made of SPAGHETTI that's ON FIRE and SINGING.

üí° The Rule: BORING = FORGOTTEN, WILD = REMEMBERED

Your brain filters out boring information as "not important." But WILD, STRANGE, VIVID images? Your brain says "THIS IS IMPORTANT! REMEMBER IT!"

Pro tip: Use MULTIPLE SCOPE tools at once. A HUGE, PURPLE, DANCING, SINGING PIANO is WAY more memorable than just a purple piano.
