About This Course

This course gives you the essential English phrases for business meetings. From small talk at the start to follow-up emails after, you'll learn practical language you can use immediately.

Each lesson teaches 5 key phrases with real examples, practice activities, and speaking challenges. By the end, you'll have 30 professional phrases ready for any meeting situation.
