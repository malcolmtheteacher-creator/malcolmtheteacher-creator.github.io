Advanced Series - Prerequisites

This is the advanced continuation of Speaking Naturally. We recommend completing the Speaking Naturally Basic Series first (6 lessons covering agreement, storytelling, advice, small talk, complaints, and emotions) or having equivalent B2-C1 level conversational skills.

About This Advanced Series

Building on the foundation of Speaking Naturally 1.0, this advanced series tackles sophisticated conversation skills for C1-C2 learners. You'll master tactful communication, complex argumentation, and the discourse markers that make you sound truly fluent.

What Makes This Series Different?

  • 3x5 Flip Card System: Learn on the front, practice on the back with 3 progressive levels
  • Built-in Spaced Repetition: Expressions from earlier lessons systematically reviewed
  • Rosenshine's Principles: Research-backed teaching methodology integrated throughout
  • Progress Tracking: Visual indicators show mastery (gray → yellow → green)
  • Expression Tracker: Real-time tracking of which phrases you use during speaking
  • Support Deck: Help available during timed activities without breaking flow
  • Structured Assessment: Clear rubrics for teacher and self-evaluation

Built on Research-Backed Pedagogy

This advanced series integrates Rosenshine's 10 Principles of Instruction, cognitive science research on spaced repetition and retrieval practice, and best practices from CELTA teacher training—all adapted specifically for advanced conversation skills.

📚

Rosenshine's Principles

Daily review, small steps, questioning, models, guided practice, checking understanding, high success rate, scaffolds, independent practice, and systematic review.

🧠

Cognitive Science

Spaced repetition (Ebbinghaus), retrieval practice (Bjork), dual coding (Paivio), cognitive load management (Sweller), and desirable difficulties.

🎓

CELTA Best Practices

Student-centered learning, minimal teacher talk time, guided discovery, contextualization, and clear lesson staging—critically adapted for advanced learners.
